# MyTummy  
*A pregnancy tracker and insight provider*

## Overview
Tracking health data can often feel tedious and time-consuming for most users. However, an important exception exists: expectant mothers, who are often highly engaged in monitoring their health and the well-being of their baby.

**MyTummy** is designed specifically for this audience—making it easier to log health information while transforming that data into clear, meaningful insights.

## Purpose
MyTummy serves two primary goals:

1. **Empower Expectant Mothers**  
   - Provide a simple and intuitive way to log health-related information  
   - Deliver personalized insights in an easy-to-understand format  
   - Complement (not replace) professional medical advice  

2. **Support Healthcare Providers**  
   - Offer more consistent and structured patient data  
   - Improve understanding of patient health trends over time  
   - Enable more informed and data-driven care  

## Key Features (Planned / Conceptual)
- Easy daily or weekly health logging  
- Insight generation based on tracked data  
- Clear visualizations of trends over time  
- Exportable summaries for healthcare visits  

## Vision
MyTummy aims to bridge the gap between day-to-day health tracking and clinical care—helping mothers feel more informed and helping providers make better decisions with richer data.

## Disclaimer
MyTummy is intended to supplement professional medical advice. Users should always consult with qualified healthcare providers regarding any medical concerns.
